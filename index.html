welcome, mama

Motherhood doesn't have to be perfect to be beautiful.

The Messy Tribe is a safe space for moms and their children to connect, slow down, and grow together — through creative, grounding, heart-centered weekly experiences.

what we hold close

Five things we believe with our whole hearts

These aren't rules. They're the soft ground this tribe is built on.

Connection

The bond between a mother and her child is sacred. Everything we create deepens it — to each other, to nature, to this very moment.

Authenticity

We celebrate real life: the mess, the tears, the laughter, and the in-between. We never pretend perfection.

Growth

Emotionally, creatively, spiritually — we hold space for both mother and child to evolve together.

Slowness

In a culture of rush, we choose presence. Breathe. Notice. Experience life with intention.

Courage

Showing up vulnerably takes courage. We honour it in every mother who joins this tribe.

the heart behind it

Hi, I'm Carissa.

If The Messy Tribe were a person, she'd be the kind of mother who invites you in for tea without apologising for the crayon marks on the table. Warm, wise, a little wild, deeply grounded — and entirely herself.

That's the space I wanted to build. Not another place telling you to do more, be more, hustle harder. A tribe where the mess is welcome, where small steps matter deeply, and where you and your little ones get to slow down and make something together.
